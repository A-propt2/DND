Travel Pace
Adventurers can move at three paces:



PaceMinuteHourDayEffect
Fast400 feet4 miles30 miles-5 penalty to passive Wisdom (Perception)
Normal300 feet3 miles24 miles—
Slow200 feet2 miles18 milesAble to use stealth


Forced March - Beyond 8 hours/day: each extra hour, make a Constitution saving throw (DC 10 + 1 per hour past 8) or suffer one level of exhaustion.
Mounts - Can gallop for ~1 hour, covering twice the fast pace distance.
Difficult Terrain - Move at half speed (1 foot of movement costs 2 feet of speed).

S p e c i a l M o v e m e n t
Climbing / Swimming - Costs 1 extra foot per foot moved (2 extra in difficult terrain), unless you have a climbing/swimming speed.
Long Jump - Cover feet equal to your Strength score (running), or half that (standing).
High Jump - Leap 3 + Strength modifier feet high (running), or half that (standing).

A c t i v i t y W h i l e T r a v e l i n g
Marching Order - Party should establish front, middle, and back ranks for traps, spotting, and combat.
Stealth - Only possible at slow pace and when not in the open.
Noticing Threats - Use passive Wisdom (Perception); fast pace imposes a -5 penalty.
Characters not watching for danger can instead:
• Navigate – Wisdom (Survival) check to avoid getting lost
• Draw a Map – No check required
• Track – Wisdom (Survival) check to follow another creature
• Forage – Wisdom (Survival) check to find food and water

T h e E n v i r o n m e n t

Falling - Take 1d6 bludgeoning damage per 10 feet fallen, max 20d6. Land prone unless damage is avoided.

Suffocating - Can hold breath for 1 + Constitution modifier minutes (min 30 seconds). After that, survive for Constitution modifier rounds (min 1), then drop to 0 hit points.

Vision and Light
• Bright Light – Normal vision for most creatures
• Dim Light – Lightly obscured; disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks relying on sight
• Darkness – Heavily obscured; effectively blinded

Special Vision Types:
• Blindsight – Perceive surroundings without sight within a specific radius
• Darkvision – See in darkness as if it were dim light (no color, only shades of gray)
• Truesight – See in magical darkness, see invisible creatures, detect illusions, see original forms of shapechangers, and see into the Ethereal Plane

Food and Water
• Food: Need 1 lb/day. Can go without food for 3 + Constitution modifier days before gaining exhaustion.
• Water: Need 1 gallon/day (2 gallons in hot weather) or suffer exhaustion.

Interacting with Objects
Tell the DM what your character does; the DM describes results. Objects can be damaged by weapons and spells — they automatically fail Strength and Dexterity saves and are immune to effects requiring other saves. At 0 hit points, an object breaks.

R e s t i n g

Short Rest - At least 1 hour of light activity (eating, drinking, tending wounds).
• Spend one or more Hit Dice (up to your level's maximum)
• Roll each die + Constitution modifier to regain hit points

Long Rest - At least 8 hours of sleep or light activity (max 2 hours of watch).
• Regain all lost hit points
• Regain spent Hit Dice up to half your total
• Can only benefit from one long rest per 24 hours
• Must have at least 1 hit point to start the rest

B e t w e e n A d v e n t u r e s

Downtime Activities - At least 8 hours/day must be spent on the activity. Days don't need to be consecutive.

C r a f t i n g - Must be proficient with relevant tools.
• Craft items worth up to 5 gp per day of downtime, spending raw materials worth half the market value
• Multiple characters can collaborate if all have the required proficiency
• Maintain a modest lifestyle for free while crafting

P r a c t i c i n g a P r o f e s s i o n - Work to maintain a modest lifestyle without paying 1 gp/day. Guild/temple members can earn a comfortable lifestyle. Performance-proficient characters earn a wealthy lifestyle.

R e c u p e r a t i n g - After 3 days of downtime, make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw to either end one effect blocking hit point recovery, or gain advantage on saves against one disease or poison for 24 hours.

R e s e a r c h i n g - Spend downtime in libraries or gathering rumors. Costs 1 gp per day. The DM determines availability, time required, and any ability checks needed (such as Intelligence (Investigation) or Charisma (Persuasion)).

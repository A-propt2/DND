Fighter:

General Characteristics:
Masters of martial combat, skilled with a variety of weapons and armor
Unparalleled mastery with weapons and armor, thorough knowledge of combat
The most diverse class — knights, soldiers, gladiators, mercenaries, and more
Combine general combat ability with a specialized Martial Archetype
Well acquainted with death, both meting it out and defying it


Class Features

H i t P o i n t s
Hit Dice: 1d10 per fighter level
1st Level: 10 + your Constitution modifier
Higher Levels: 1d10 + your Constitution modifier after every level

P r o f i c i e n c i e s
Armor: All armor, shields
Weapons: Simple weapons, martial weapons
Saving Throws: Strength, Constitution
Skills: Choose two from Acrobatics, Animal Handling, Athletics, History, Insight, Intimidation, Perception, and Survival
Starting Equipment:
• (a) chain mail or (b) leather, longbow, and 20 arrows
• (a) a martial weapon and a shield or (b) two martial weapons
• (a) a light crossbow and 20 bolts or (b) two handaxes
• (a) a dungeoneer's pack or (b) an explorer's pack

Leveling: Gains extra ability score improvements compared to other classes (at 4th, 6th, 8th, 12th, 14th, 16th, 19th level).

F i g h t i n g S t y l e - Choose a specialty at 1st level: Archery (+2 to ranged attack rolls), Defense (+1 AC while wearing armor), Dueling (+2 damage with one-handed melee weapon), Great Weapon Fighting (reroll 1s and 2s on damage dice for two-handed weapons), Protection (impose disadvantage on attacks against others when wielding a shield), or Two-Weapon Fighting (add ability modifier to second attack damage).

S e c o n d W i n d - As a bonus action, regain 1d10 + fighter level HP. Once per short or long rest.

A c t i o n S u r g e - From 2nd level, take one extra action on your turn. Once per short or long rest. Two uses per rest at 17th level.

M a r t i a l A r c h e t y p e - At 3rd level, choose: Champion, Battle Master, or Eldritch Knight. Features at 3rd, 7th, 10th, 15th, and 18th level.

E x t r a A t t a c k - From 5th level, attack twice. Three times at 11th level, four times at 20th level.

I n d o m i t a b l e - From 9th level, reroll a failed saving throw (must use new result). Once per long rest. Two uses at 13th, three at 17th.

C h a m p i o n:
Raw physical power honed to deadly perfection.
Improved Critical - From 3rd: critical hit on a roll of 19–20.
Remarkable Athlete - From 7th: add half proficiency bonus to Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution checks not already using it.
Additional Fighting Style - At 10th: choose a second Fighting Style option.
Superior Critical - From 15th: critical hit on a roll of 18–20.
Survivor - From 18th: at the start of each turn, regain HP equal to 5 + Constitution modifier if you have half or fewer HP.

B a t t l e M a s t e r:
Combat is an academic discipline — use maneuvers fueled by superiority dice.
Combat Superiority - From 3rd: know 3 maneuvers and have 4 superiority dice (d8). Gain 2 more maneuvers at 7th, 10th, 15th. Additional die at 7th and 15th. Dice become d10 at 10th level, d12 at 18th.
Student of War - At 3rd: proficiency with one type of artisan's tools.
Know Your Enemy - From 7th: after 1 minute of observation, learn how a creature compares to you in 2 characteristics.
Relentless - From 15th: regain 1 superiority die if you roll initiative with none remaining.

E l d r i t c h K n i g h t:
Combines martial mastery with arcane study (abjuration and evocation spells from the wizard list).

Spellcasting - From 3rd: Intelligence is your spellcasting ability. Limited spell slots (up to 4th level spells).
Weapon Bond - From 3rd: bond with up to 2 weapons via 1-hour ritual. Can't be disarmed; summon them as a bonus action.
War Magic - From 7th: when you cast a cantrip, make one weapon attack as a bonus action.
Eldritch Strike - At 10th: when you hit a creature, it has disadvantage on the next saving throw against your spells before end of your next turn.
Arcane Charge - At 15th: when you use Action Surge, teleport up to 30 feet.
Improved War Magic - From 18th: when you cast any spell (not just cantrips), make one weapon attack as a bonus action.

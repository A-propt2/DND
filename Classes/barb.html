Starting equipment:
• (a) a greataxe or (b) any martial melee weapon
• (a) two handaxes or (b) any simple weapon

Leveling:

R a g e - In battle on your turn, you can enter a rage as a bonus action. While raging, you gain the following benefits if you aren’t wearing heavy armor:
• You have advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws.
• When you make a melee weapon attack using Strength, you gain a bonus to the damage roll(look at the table).
• You have resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.
• You can’t cast spells or concentrate on them while raging.
• Your rage lasts for 1 minute. It ends if you go uunconscious or if dont attack or get hit in a turn.
• Once you use all your rages (look at lvl chart) you must finish a long rest before you can rage again.

U n a r m o r e d D e f e n s e - While not wearing any armor(shields don't count), your Armor Class equals 10 + your Dexterity + your Constitution.

R e c k l e s s A t t a c k - From 2nd level when you make your first attack, you can attack recklessly, you get advantage on melee weapon attack rolls using Strength during this turn, but attacks against you have advantage until your next turn.

D a n g e r S e n s e - From 2nd level, you get a sense of danger You have advantage on Dexterity saving throws against things you can see(such as traps and spells). dosent work when blinded, deafened, or incapacitated.

F r e n z y - From 3rd level, you can go into a frenzy when you rage. During then you can make a single melee weapon attack as a bonus action. When your rage ends, you suffer one level of exhaustion.
M in d l e s s R a g e - from 6th level, you can’t be charmed or frightened while raging.

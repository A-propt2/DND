Paladin:

General Characteristics:
Holy warriors bound to a sacred oath to stand against the forces of evil
Power comes from a commitment to justice itself, not just from a god
Train for years to master combat, but their magic is their true strength
Can heal the sick, smite the wicked, and protect the innocent
Almost never evil in alignment; most walk paths of charity and justice


Class Features

H i t P o i n t s
Hit Dice: 1d10 per paladin level
1st Level: 10 + your Constitution modifier
Higher Levels: 1d10 + your Constitution modifier after every level

P r o f i c i e n c i e s
Armor: All armor, shields
Weapons: Simple weapons, martial weapons
Saving Throws: Wisdom, Charisma
Skills: Choose two from Athletics, Insight, Intimidation, Medicine, Persuasion, and Religion
Starting Equipment:
• (a) a martial weapon and a shield or (b) two martial weapons
• (a) five javelins or (b) any simple melee weapon
• (a) a priest's pack or (b) an explorer's pack
• Chain mail and a holy symbol

D i v i n e S e n s e - As an action, until end of your next turn, know the location of any celestial, fiend, or undead within 60 feet not behind total cover. Also detect consecrated/desecrated places. Uses equal to 1 + Charisma modifier per long rest.

L a y o n H a n d s - Blessed touch can heal wounds. Pool of HP equal to paladin level × 5, replenished on long rest. As an action, touch a creature to restore HP from the pool. Spend 5 HP to cure one disease or neutralize one poison instead. No effect on undead or constructs.

F i g h t i n g S t y l e - At 2nd level, choose: Defense (+1 AC in armor), Dueling (+2 damage one-handed), Great Weapon Fighting (reroll 1s and 2s on two-handed damage), or Protection (impose disadvantage on attacks against nearby allies when wielding a shield).

S p e l l c a s t i n g - From 2nd level, Charisma is your spellcasting ability. Prepare spells equal to Charisma modifier + half paladin level (rounded down). Spell slots up to 5th level by 20th level. Can cast prepared paladin spells using a holy symbol focus.

D i v i n e S m i t e - From 2nd level, when you hit with a melee weapon attack, expend a spell slot to deal 2d8 radiant damage + 1d8 per spell level above 1st (max 5d8). Extra 1d8 against undead or fiends.

D i v i n e H e a l t h - By 3rd level, immune to disease.

S a c r e d O a t h - At 3rd level, swear your oath and choose: Oath of Devotion, Oath of the Ancients, or Oath of Vengeance. Grants oath spells (always prepared), Channel Divinity, and features at 3rd, 7th, 15th, and 20th level.

C h a n n e l D i v i n i t y - From the Sacred Oath at 3rd level. Once per short or long rest.

E x t r a A t t a c k - From 5th level, attack twice when taking the Attack action.

A u r a o f P r o t e c t i o n - From 6th level, you and friendly creatures within 10 feet add your Charisma modifier to saving throws (min +1). Range increases to 30 feet at 18th level.

A u r a o f C o u r a g e - From 10th level, you and friendly creatures within 10 feet can't be frightened while you are conscious. Range increases to 30 feet at 18th level.

I m p r o v e d D i v i n e S m i t e - From 11th level, all melee weapon hits deal an extra 1d8 radiant damage.

C l e a n s i n g T o u c h - From 14th level, as an action end one spell on yourself or a willing creature you touch. Uses equal to Charisma modifier per long rest.

O a t h o f D e v o t i o n:
The knight in shining armor — honesty, courage, compassion, honor, duty.
Oath Spells: 3rd - protection from evil and good, sanctuary. 5th - lesser restoration, zone of truth. 9th - beacon of hope, dispel magic. 13th - freedom of movement, guardian of faith. 17th - commune, flame strike.
Sacred Weapon - Add Charisma modifier to attack rolls with one weapon for 1 minute, weapon counts as magic damage now; weapon emits bright light 20 ft.
Turn the Unholy - Each fiend or undead within 30 feet must succeed on a Wisdom save or be turned for 1 minute.
Aura of Devotion - From 7th: you and friendly creatures within 10 ft can't be charmed. (30 ft at 18th)
Purity of Spirit - From 15th: always under the effects of protection from evil and good.
Holy Nimbus - At 20th: you can emit 1-minute aura of sunlight — enemies in it take 10 radiant damage at start of their turn; advantage on saves against fiend/undead spells.

O a t h o f t h e A n c i e n t s:
Fey knights who love the beautiful and life-giving things of the world - Kindle the Light. Shelter the Light. Preserve Your Own Light. Be the Light.
Oath Spells: 3rd - ensnaring strike, speak with animals. 5th - moonbeam, misty step. 9th - plant growth, protection from energy. 13th - ice storm, stoneskin. 17th - commune with nature, tree stride.
Nature's Wrath - Spectral vines restrain a creature within 10 feet (Str or Dex save on each turn).
Turn the Faithless - Fey and fiends within 30 feet must succeed on a Wisdom save or be turned for 1 minute (true form revealed).
Aura of Warding - From 7th: you and friendly creatures within 10 ft have resistance to spell damage. (30 ft at 18th)
Undying Sentinel - From 15th: when reduced to 0 HP (not killed outright), drop to 1 HP instead. Once per long rest. Also immune to magical aging.
Elder Champion - At 20th: for 1 minute, regain 10 HP at start of each turn, cast paladin spells as bonus actions, enemies within 10 ft have disadvantage on saves against your spells.

O a t h o f V e n g e a n c e:
Avengers — punish those who have committed grievous sins by any means necessary-Fight the Greater Evil. No Mercy for the Wicked. By Any Means Necessary. Restitution.
Oath Spells: 3rd - bane, hunter's mark. 5th - hold person, misty step. 9th - haste, protection from energy. 13th - banishment, dimension door. 17th - hold monster, scrying.
Abjure Enemy - One creature within 60 ft must make a Wisdom save (fiends/undead have disadvantage) or be frightened (speed 0) for 1 minute. On success, speed is halved for 1 minute.
Vow of Enmity - As a bonus action, gain advantage on attack rolls against one creature within 10 feet for 1 minute or until it drops to 0 HP.
Relentless Avenger - From 7th: Stop enemies from retreating and when you hit with an opportunity attack, move up to half speed as part of the same reaction without provoking opportunity attacks.
Soul of Vengeance - From 15th: when a creature under your Vow of Enmity attacks, use your reaction to make a melee attack against it.
Avenging Angel - At 20th: for 1 hour, gain wings (fly 60 ft) and emit a 30-foot aura of menace — enemies that enter must succeed on a Wisdom save or be frightened for 1 minute(you gain advantage on attack rolls).

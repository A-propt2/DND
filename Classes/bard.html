Bard:

General charecteristics:
Charming performers who use magic through art
Masters of music, speech, and storytelling
Support allies and manipulate enemies
Travelers who gather tales and knowledge

Class Features

H i t P o in t s
Hit Dice: 1d 8 per bard level
1st Level: 1d 8 + your Constitution
Higher Levels: 1d 8 + your Constitution after every lvl

P r o f ic i e n c i e s
Armor: Light armor
Weapons: Simple weapons, hand crossbows, longswords, rapiers, shortswords
Tools: Three musical instruments of your choice
Saving Throws: Dexterity, Charisma
Skills: Choose any three
Starting equipment:
• (a) a rapier, (b) a longsword, or (c) any simple weapon
• (a) a diplomat’s pack or (b) an entertainer’s pack
• (a) a lute or (b) any other musical instrument
• Leather armor and a dagger

Leveling:

S p e l l c a s t i n g - You can cast spells using Charisma as your spellcasting ability.
• You know a number of spells and cantrips (see lvl table).
• Spell save DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Charisma. Spell attack modifier = your proficiency bonus + your Charisma .
• You can use a musical instrument as a spellcasting focus.
• You regain all spell slots when you finish a long rest.

B a r d i c I n s p i r a t i o n - From 1st level, you can inspire others using a bonus action.
• A creature within 60 feet gets an inspiration die (starts as d6).
• They can add it to ability checks, attack rolls, or saving throws.
• Number of uses are equal to your Charisma modifier.
• You regain uses after a long rest.

J a c k o f A l l T r a d e s - From 2nd level, you add half your proficiency bonus to any ability check you aren’t proficient in.

S o n g o f R e s t - From 2nd level, during a short rest, you and allies regain extra hit points if they hear your performance.

C o l l e g e o f L o r e: Masters of knowledge and sharp wit.
B o n u s P r o f i c i e n c i e s - From 3rd level, you gain three extra skills.
C u t t i n g W o r d s - From 3rd level, you can use your reaction to subtract your inspiration die from an enemy’s(that can hear you and isnt immune to being charmed) attack, ability check, or damage roll.
A d d i t i o n a l M a g i c a l S e c r e t s - From 6th level, you learn extra spells from any class or cantrips(dont count as bard spells).
P e e r l e s s S k i l l - From 14th level, you can add your inspiration die to your own ability checks.

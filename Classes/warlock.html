Warlock:

General Characteristics:
Wielders of magic derived from a bargain with an extraplanar entity (patron)
Driven by an insatiable need for knowledge and power
Supplement their magic with facility at hand-to-hand combat
Recover spell slots on a short rest, but have very few of them
Gain powerful Eldritch Invocations and a Pact Boon from their patron


Class Features

H i t P o i n t s
Hit Dice: 1d8 per warlock level
1st Level: 8 + your Constitution modifier
Higher Levels: 1d8 + your Constitution modifier after every level

P r o f i c i e n c i e s
Armor: Light armor
Weapons: Simple weapons
Saving Throws: Wisdom, Charisma
Skills: Choose two from Arcana, Deception, History, Intimidation, Investigation, Nature, and Religion
Starting Equipment:
• (a) a light crossbow and 20 bolts or (b) any simple weapon
• (a) a component pouch or (b) an arcane focus
• (a) a scholar's pack or (b) a dungeoneer's pack
• Leather armor, any simple weapon, and two daggers

Leveling: Warlocks have very few spell slots but all are the same level. Regain all slots on short or long rest. See Warlock table for slot count and level.

O t h e r w o r l d l y P a t r o n - At 1st level, choose your patron: The Archfey, The Fiend, or The Great Old One. Grants an expanded spell list, features at 1st, 6th, 10th, and 14th level, and shapes the nature of your pact.

P a c t M a g i c - Charisma is your spellcasting ability. Know 2 cantrips and spells as shown in the Warlock table. All spell slots are the same level (scaling from 1st to 5th as you level). Regain all expended slots on a short or long rest.

E l d r i t c h I n v o c a t i o n s - From 2nd level, gain 2 invocations from the warlock list. Gain more as shown. Can replace one each level up.

P a c t B o o n - At 3rd level, your patron bestows a gift. Choose one:
• Pact of the Chain - Learn find familiar as a ritual. Your familiar can take special forms (imp, pseudodragon, quasit, or sprite). You can forgo one attack to let it attack.
• Pact of the Blade - As an action, create a melee pact weapon (any form you choose) in your hand. It's magical and counts as your weapon. You can bond a magic weapon to become your pact weapon. Summon it as a bonus action.
• Pact of the Tome - Your patron gives you a Book of Shadows containing 3 cantrips from any class's spell list (cast at will, not counting against your cantrips known).

M y s t i c A r c a n u m - From 11th level, choose one spell from your warlock spell list of a higher level than your slot level. Cast it once without expending a spell slot. Regain on long rest. Gain a 7th-level arcanum at 13th, 8th-level at 15th, 9th-level at 17th.

E l d r i t c h M a s t e r - At 20th level, spend 1 minute entreating your patron to regain all expended spell slots. Once per long rest.

T h e A r c h f e y:
A lord or lady of the Fey — capricious, powerful, and ancient.
Expanded spells: 1st - faerie fire, sleep. 2nd - calm emotions, phantasmal force. 3rd - blink, plant growth. 4th - dominate beast, greater invisibility. 5th - dominate person, seeming.
Fey Presence (1st): as an action, creatures in a 10-foot cube originating from you make a Wisdom save or be charmed or frightened (your choice) until end of next turn. Recharges on short or long rest.
Misty Escape (6th): when you take damage, use your reaction to turn invisible and teleport up to 60 feet. Remain invisible until start of your next turn or until you attack/cast. Recharges on short or long rest.
Beguiling Defenses (10th): immune to charm. When a creature attempts to charm you, use your reaction to try to turn the charm back on it (Wisdom save or be charmed by you for 1 minute).
Dark Delirium (14th): as an action, spend... choose a creature within 60 ft. It makes a Wisdom save or be charmed or frightened for 1 minute (concentration). The creature thinks it is lost in a misty realm of your design. Recharges on short or long rest.

T h e F i e n d:
A powerful devil or demon from the lower planes — evil aims, destructive power.
Expanded spells: 1st - burning hands, command. 2nd - blindness/deafness, scorching ray. 3rd - fireball, stinking cloud. 4th - fire shield, wall of fire. 5th - flame strike, hallow.
Dark One's Blessing (1st): when you reduce a hostile creature to 0 HP, gain temporary HP equal to Charisma modifier + warlock level.
Dark One's Own Luck (6th): once per short or long rest, when you make an ability check or saving throw, add a d10 to the roll (after seeing it, before effects apply).
Fiendish Resilience (10th): after a short or long rest, choose one damage type and gain resistance to it until you choose differently. Magical or silver weapons ignore this.
Hurl Through Hell (14th): once per long rest, when you hit a creature, instantly send it through the lower planes. It returns at the end of your next turn. If it's not a fiend, it takes 10d10 psychic damage.

T h e G r e a t O l d O n e:
An utterly alien entity from the Far Realm — incomprehensible motives, ancient knowledge.
Expanded spells: 1st - dissonant whispers, Tasha's hideous laughter. 2nd - detect thoughts, phantasmal force. 3rd - clairvoyance, sending. 4th - dominate beast, Evard's black tentacles. 5th - dominate person, telekinesis.
Awakened Mind (1st): telepathically communicate with any creature you can see within 30 feet (doesn't require a shared language, but the creature must understand at least one language).
Entropic Ward (6th): when a creature makes an attack roll against you, use your reaction to impose disadvantage. If it misses, you have advantage on your next attack against it before end of next turn. Recharges on short or long rest.
Thought Shield (10th): your thoughts can't be read. Resistance to psychic damage. When a creature deals psychic damage to you, it takes the same amount.
Create Thrall (14th): as an action, touch an incapacitated humanoid. It's charmed by you until remove curse is cast on it. You can communicate telepathically with it while on the same plane.
